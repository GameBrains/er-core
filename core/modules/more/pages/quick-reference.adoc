== Orders and conditions

A summary of orders and conditions -- for full detail see xref:main-rules:orders-and-conditions.adoc[]

image:main-rules:marker-advance-orders.svg[role="left float-gap",width=40,height=40]
*Advance* orders (default):
May move in the Movement phase.
May shoot in the Shooting phase.
May *not* move in the Assault phase.

image:main-rules:marker-assault-orders.svg[role="left float-gap",width=40,height=40]
*Assault* orders:
May move in the Movement phase.
May shoot in the Shooting phase, at *half* effect.
*Must* move in the Assault phase, towards the enemy, and can initiate an assault.

image:main-rules:marker-march-orders.svg[role="left float-gap",width=40,height=40]
*March* orders:
May *triple* move in the Movement phase.
May *not* shoot in the Shooting phase; opponent uses the Marching column on the Firepower table.
May *not* move in the Assault phase; if engaged by the enemy then fight at *half* effect.

image:main-rules:marker-overwatch-orders.svg[role="left float-gap",width=40,height=40]
*Overwatch* orders:
May move only *5 cm* in the Movement phase.
May shoot in the Shooting phase, and *re-roll misses*.
May *not* move in the Assault phase.

[.float-group]
--
image:main-rules:marker-special-orders.svg[role="left float-gap",width=40,height=40]
*Special* orders:
Indicates that special orders are in effect, such as xref:special-rules:flak.adoc[] or xref:special-rules:artillery.adoc#preparatory-bombardment[Preparatory Bombardment].
--

image:main-rules:marker-broken-condition.svg[role="left float-gap",width=40,height=40]
*Broken* condition:
May not use orders.
May make *only* a 20 cm xref:main-rules:broken-detachments.adoc#retreat-move[Retreat move] in the Movement phase.
May *not* shoot in the Shooting phase.
May *not* move in the Assault phase (aside from the initial Retreat move after losing an assault); if engaged by the enemy, fight at *half* effect.

image:main-rules:marker-immobilised-condition.svg[role="left float-gap",width=40,height=40]
*Immobilised* condition:
May not use orders.
May *not* move in the Movement phase.
May shoot in the Shooting phase; opponent uses the Immobilised column on the Firepower table.
May *not* move in the Assault phase.
