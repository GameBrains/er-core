= Deathstrike Missile

A powerful one-shot missile.
Though not as devastating as a {vortex-missile}, it is a weapon that inspires justifiable dread.

---

When you make a {deathstrike-missile} shot:

. Place a Barrage template in the standard way.
. For each unit that is covered by the template, roll a D6 and resolve this many {macro-weapon} shots against it.
